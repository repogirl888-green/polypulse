PolyPulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

PolyPulse ("the app") is a live trend tracker that displays publicly available prediction-market data. This policy explains what information the app does and does not handle.

What we collect: nothing

PolyPulse does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

How the app works

When you open PolyPulse, your device requests publicly available market data directly from Polymarket's public Gamma API (gamma-api.polymarket.com). This is an ordinary web request, like loading a public webpage. PolyPulse does not send any information about you along with it, and no data about your activity is retained by us.

Local storage on your device

The app uses a service worker to cache its own interface files on your device so it loads quickly and works offline. This cache contains only the app's own files — never personal data — and you can clear it at any time by clearing your browser or app storage.

Third-party services

Market data is provided by Polymarket's public API. If you tap a market card, you may be taken to polymarket.com in your browser, where Polymarket's own privacy policy applies. PolyPulse is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Polymarket.

Children's privacy

PolyPulse does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children. Because the app displays prediction-market (event-odds) information, it is intended for adult users.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new "last updated" date.
